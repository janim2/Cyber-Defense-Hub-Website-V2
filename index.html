About Us

Cyber Defence Hub is a non-governmental organization dedicated to advancing cybersecurity, digital forensics, and policy advocacy on a global scale. In a world of growing cyber threats, we bring together professionals, researchers, educators, and policymakers to enhance digital safety and security.

  • Our Vision

    A safer digital world where individuals, organizations, and communities can thrive without fear of cyber threats, supported by education, collaboration, and ethical cybersecurity principles.

  • Our Mission

    To protect individuals, communities, organizations, and critical infrastructure from cyber threats while promoting responsible digital citizenship and cybersecurity awareness worldwide.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are our most common questions.

What types of training programs do you offer?

We offer a range of programs from basic cybersecurity awareness workshops to advanced technical training in digital forensics and incident response.

How can organizations benefit from your services?

Organizations can access our expertise for security assessments, incident response, staff training, and policy development to enhance their cybersecurity posture.

Do you offer assistance to individuals affected by cyber crimes?

Yes, we provide support and guidance to individuals who have experienced cyber attacks, including incident response and recovery assistance.

How can I get involved with Cyber Defence Hub?

There are multiple ways to engage with us, including volunteering, partnerships, and participating in our training programs.

What sets Cyber Defence Hub apart from other cybersecurity organizations?

As a non-governmental organization, we uniquely combine technical expertise with public service, making cybersecurity accessible to all while influencing policy through strong partnerships with industry, government, and academia.
